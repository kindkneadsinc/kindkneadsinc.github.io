About

Kind Kneads is an on-site chair massage social enterprise, which uses partial proceeds to spread local acts of kindness in HRM & throughout NS. Bookings are available for special events such as employee/customer appreciation days, corporate wellness events, sales incentive rewards, trade shows/expos, conferences, holiday parties, and recurring in-office contracts.
